How to work with program

  1. Enter data at the points: A,B,C,D.
  2. Then click Reset or Create, and a canvas of points will be created, which you can move and change the radius.
  3. It is possible to change the radius of the circle dynamically, if you try to drag the point that is on the edge, the circle will change its radius.
  4. If you click on the Open Modal button, a modal window will open with information about all the points, their coordinates, radius and color.

As a result, you will have between 0 and 6 points in the client area, depending on the points entered. If you do not enter some data, no point will be created.
